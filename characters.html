Captain America

  • During the war in 1941, Steve Rogers wanted to join the America’s armed forces, but they rejected him because of his small stature. However, his ambition of defending his country got him in to an experimental program that turns him in to a super soldier called Captain America. For less then a year he help an agency called S.H.I.E.L.D defeat Hydra. However his final mission he crashed in the Arctic to hide the tesseract from Hydra. In the early 21st century he woke up to find out that he was was encased in ice for sixty-six years. His ongoing love interest is Peggy Carter and his all time best friend is bucky barnes (Winter soldier).

Iron Man

  • A billionaire industrialist and genius inventor, Tony Stark was working overseas when he was kidnapped and forced to make nuclear weapon. Instead he made the iron man suit to make an escape. During his time in capture his cellmate named Yinsen who is a doctor implants an electromagnet in to Stark’s chest to keep the shrapnel shards that wounded him from reaching his heart. When he returns to America he modifies the suit to help with crime and terrorism.

Black Widow

  • Black Widow’s real name is Natalia Alianovna Romanova. She is a Russian Spy, and was introduced as an antagonist in one of the Iron man Comic books. She is trained to be a spy, martial artist, and sniper. Later she starts to freelance as a international agent for a espionage group called S.H.I.E.L.D. At a young age she was thrusted in to a life of an assassin.

Hawkeye

  • Clinton Francis Barton otherwise known as Hawkeye. At a young age he lost both of his parents. Six years later him an his brother Barney run away and end up at a Carnival. Where he caught the eye of a swordsman who took Clint under his wing. With the help of Trickshot and the Swordsman he was trained to become a master archer.

Hulk

  • The Hulk rather known as Bruce Banner. At Culver University in Virginia, Banner met General Thaddeus. He had a theory of making humans immune to gamma radiation. This was part of the World War II “super soldier” program that created Captain America. The exposure to gamma radiation transforms Banner in to hulk.

Spiderman

  • Spiderman otherwise known as Peter Parker, is bitten by a genetically altered spiders. He gains super strength an spider like abilities to cling to any surface. The death of his Uncle Ben made him vow that he will fight crime and make sure he understands what his Uncle Ben told him before he died.”With great power comes great responsibility.”
